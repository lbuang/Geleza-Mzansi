About Us

What is G.M.O.A ?

Geleza Mzansi Online Application is a product of Unrealistic Telemachanics with goals of equalizing opportunities of access to qualify education of the motherland and take South African education to a Global level.

We operate br expanding paths of entry to tertiary by means of making it simple for South African pupils to showcase their good grades to their dream Colleges and Universities.With the aim of cutting costs of applying to tertiary, and delivering the applications in time and give everyone an equal chance to access global level education.

We encourage the doing of everything now, sometimes "later" becomes "never"!

Why Us ?

Simplified Online Application


We offer the simplest form of online application process to any higher education and training institution in the country! Our applying system is developed to cater even for uGogo if she wants to further her education ngoba Imfundo kayi gugelwa.

Career Guidance


At G.M.O.A, one or group career guidance is available in our WhatsApp community to help pupils make informed decisions when choosing what course to apply for.

Pupil - Assistance Application


At G.M.O.A it is possible to get a direct assistant for each pupil to assist in the application process for those who do not have access to Internet services/connections to apply using our friendly online system.
